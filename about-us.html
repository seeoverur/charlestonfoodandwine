About Our Festival

Charleston's premier food and wine festival celebrates the Lowcountry's rich culinary heritage while showcasing innovative approaches to Southern cuisine.

Our Mission

We bring together the finest chefs, winemakers, food artisans, and culinary enthusiasts from across the region to celebrate exceptional food, sustainable practices, and community connection. Our annual event creates a platform where local producers meet passionate food lovers, fostering relationships that strengthen Charleston's vibrant culinary ecosystem.

What We Offer

Each year, our festival features dozens of tasting stations, cooking demonstrations, wine seminars, and interactive culinary experiences. Attendees sample signature dishes from award-winning restaurants, discover emerging food trends, and learn directly from master chefs about techniques and ingredients that define contemporary Southern cooking.

Beyond tastings, we host educational workshops covering topics from sustainable farming to artisanal food production. Live entertainment, local craft vendors, and family-friendly activities create a welcoming atmosphere for all ages.

Commitment to Sustainability

Environmental responsibility stands central to our values. We implement comprehensive recycling programs, partner with local farms practicing sustainable agriculture, and minimize waste through thoughtful planning. Our goal is to celebrate food while protecting the natural resources that make exceptional cuisine possible.

Community Impact

The festival supports Charleston's culinary community by providing exposure for emerging talent, creating networking opportunities for industry professionals, and educating the public about the importance of supporting local food systems. A portion of proceeds benefits culinary education programs and food security initiatives throughout the Lowcountry.

Join Us

Whether you're a dedicated foodie, casual diner, or simply curious about Charleston's culinary scene, our festival welcomes you. Experience the flavors, meet the people, and discover why Charleston continues to earn recognition as one of America's most exciting food destinations.

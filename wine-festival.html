Charleston Wine Festival

A wine festival showcases diverse selections from regional vineyards and producers, offering tastings and purchases in a celebratory atmosphere that combines exceptional wines with entertainment and culinary experiences.

Festival Highlights

Our events feature carefully curated wines organized by regional origins or varietals. While wine takes center stage, we enhance the experience with complementary food pairings from local culinary artisans. Sweet and savory selections perfectly match the diverse wine offerings, creating memorable flavor combinations.

Charitable auctions during the festival support local organizations, allowing attendees to contribute to the community while enjoying premium wines.

How It Works

Hundreds of wine selections from various producers are displayed throughout the venue. Knowledgeable staff pour tastings directly from barrels and bottles, accepting cash payment for purchases. Temperature-controlled storage ensures optimal serving conditions for every wine variety.

Your Visit

Upon arrival, guests receive tasting glasses for a small refundable deposit, often included with admission. Festival catalogs detail all available wines with location maps, helping you navigate selections efficiently and plan your tasting journey.

Live musical performances create an inviting ambiance that encourages guests to relax, socialize, and savor the complete wine festival experience throughout the day.
